The first Proof-of-Thought crypto-currency

No CPUs, GPUs or ASICs. Mine it only with your brain!

Motocoin is a crypto-currency of a new kind. It is inspired by Bitcoin but has major difference. Bitcoin is based on a scheme called proof-of-work. In this scheme miners perform some computations (called work) on their computers and then use proof of that work to secure coin transactions and to get their reward. Motocoin is similar to Bitcoin but instead of relying on a work done by computers it relies on a work done by humans. This work consists of playing 2D moto-simulator game, hence the currency name. People play the game and get rewarded for this. We call this scheme proof-of-thought.

Advantages:

  • No need for expensive mining hardware. To be a miner you just need an average computer and some skill at videogames.
  • No energy is wasted on useless computations.
  • It is more secure as it is almost impossible to perform 51% attack. If you control enough hardware you can perform 51% attack on a classic proof-of-work crypto-currency, machines wouldn't bore to fulfill your evil wishes. But people prefer to work for their profit and it would be too expensive to hire enough people, especially considering that most skilled players would rather mine Motocoin than destroy it.

Network parameters:

  • New block every 5 minutes.
  • Initial reward is 100 coins per block.
  • Reward is halved every 200 000 blocks (every two years) but will never be less than 2 coins per block.

Motocoin mining

Unlike other crypto-currentcies, you cannot mine Motocoin just by running some software; you will have to play the game called Motogame. This game is inspired by Elastomania, it is a 2D moto-simulator. You can control your bike using arrow keys and space. Your goal is to touch the coin with wheel or head. You need to complete any level in time less than current target time. Target time will be changed approximately every 3.5 days to sustain constant block generation speed.

Here is what you need to do to start mining:

  1. Download Motocoin client.
  2. Run it and wait until your client will synchronize with the network (this make take a while).
  3. Go to the Game tab and press Play to mine button.
  4. Keep pressing F6 until you will see a level that you think you can complete.
  5. Play the game.

When you will hit the coin you will get reward (currently 100 motocoins). There is some chance that you will not get any reward, this may happen if someone else will complete level in the same moment as you. While playing you may see sudden level changes, they happen when someone else successfully completes a level.
